HANGMAN GAME - RULES

Great to see you here Player! Welcome to HANGMAN GAME.

The object of this game is to guess the unknown word before your stick figure is hung.

You can only guess one single letter at a time and you get a total of 6 tries.

After a couple wrong guesses you get the opportunity to select your first joker which will grant you the definition of the unknown word.

After another wrong guess you unlock the second Joker which will uncover one random letter of the unknown word.

Good luck and have fun testing your vocabulary!
